SEC Preparatory

Secondary Education Certificate

Entry Requirements
  • Age: 15+ years
  • English: Grade 8 level OR placement test ≥ 50%
  • Academic: Completed Grade 8 or equivalent
  • Duration: 24 months / 2400 hours
  • Subjects: 5–8 subjects (compulsory + elective)
View SEC Details

International A Level (IAL)

CIE & Edexcel Dual Pathway

Entry Requirements
  • Age: 16+ years
  • English: O-Level C6 OR placement test ≥ 50%
  • Academic: O-Level/IGCSE/Grade 10 or equivalent
  • Duration: 24 months / 2400 hours (AS → A2)
  • Subjects: 3–5 subjects from 10 options
  • Exams: May/June & Oct/Nov sessions
