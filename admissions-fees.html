Fees & Payments

Transparent fee structure and payment policies

Fee Protection Scheme (FPS)

What is FPS?

The Fee Protection Scheme protects your tuition fees in case of school closure or non-fulfillment of course obligations. This is a mandatory requirement for all Private Education Institutions (PEIs) in Singapore.

Our Insurance Provider:

ESIC partners with Lonpac Insurance to ensure all course fees are fully protected under the FPS framework.

Protection Coverage: All tuition fees paid are covered. In the event of school closure, students will receive a full refund of unused course fees or be transferred to another institution.

Late Payment Policy
  • Due Date + 5 Days: Payment considered late
  • After 5 Working Days: Course access may be suspended
  • Continued Non-Payment: May result in enrollment cancellation

As per Student Contract Clause 2.5. School reserves final decision rights.

Student Contract & Cooling-Off Period
  • 10-Day Cooling-Off Period: 100% refund if contract cancelled within 10 calendar days of signing.
  • Contract Explanation: ESIC staff will explain course details, fees, refund terms, and FPS before signing.

Important: Students and guardians (for minors) must fully understand the contract terms before signing. All questions should be clarified during the contract explanation session.

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer: Direct transfer to ESIC's designated bank account
  • Cheque: Made payable to "Equistar International College"
  • Credit/Debit Card: Visa, MasterCard accepted (processing fees may apply)
  • PayNow: Singapore's instant payment service
Refund Policy

Refunds are processed according to the following schedule:

Withdrawal Period Refund Amount
Within 10 days of signing (Cooling-off) 100% refund
Before course commencement 90% refund (less admin fee)
Within first 10% of course duration 70% refund
After 10% of course duration No refund

Note: Application fees are non-refundable in all cases.
