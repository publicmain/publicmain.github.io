Singapore Regulations & Campus Life

Essential Compliance Guide for International Students

⚠️ Important: Compliance with Singapore regulations and Student Pass requirements is mandatory. Violations may result in pass cancellation and deportation.

Student's Pass Requirements
Mandatory Attendance & Presence
  • Attendance Rate: Minimum 90% attendance is required
  • Continuous Absence: Cannot be absent for 7 consecutive days without authorization
  • Daily Presence: Must be in school an average of 5 hours per day
  • Pass Expiry: Cannot remain in Singapore after Student's Pass expires
Study & Work Restrictions
  • Course Restrictions: Can only study at the institution and course stated on your pass
  • Employment: Working without authorization is prohibited
  • Pass Surrender: Must return Student's Pass within 7 days of course suspension or completion
Singapore Social Regulations & Laws
Littering Offenses
  • First Offense: Fine up to S$2,000
  • Repeat Offenses: Higher fines plus possible Corrective Work Order (CWO)
  • Severe Cases: Court prosecution possible

Tip: Always dispose of trash in designated bins. Keep Singapore clean!

Chewing Gum Ban
  • Import/Sale/Possession: Prohibited (except therapeutic gum from pharmacies)
  • Penalties: Fines for violation
Smoking Regulations
  • Indoor Smoking: Banned in most indoor locations
  • Legal Age: 21 years old minimum
  • Designated Areas: Smoke only in designated smoking areas
  • Penalties: Fines for smoking in prohibited areas
Drug Laws (Zero Tolerance)

⛔ SEVERE PENALTIES - DEATH PENALTY APPLICABLE

  • Possession: Mandatory jail and caning
  • Trafficking: Death penalty for specific quantities (e.g., >15g heroin, >500g cannabis)
  • Zero Tolerance: Singapore has extremely strict drug laws

Warning: Do NOT bring or use any illegal substances in Singapore.

Alcohol Regulations
  • Public Drinking Hours: Prohibited 10:30 PM - 7:00 AM in public places
  • Controlled Zones: Some areas (Little India, Geylang) have stricter restrictions on weekends
  • Legal Drinking Age: 18 years old
  • Penalties: Fines for drinking in prohibited times/areas
Daily Life Tips
  • Public Transport: MRT and buses are efficient - get an EZ-Link card
  • Dining: Hawker centers offer affordable local food
  • Respect Local Culture: Singapore is multi-cultural - be respectful of all communities
  • Queuing: Always queue orderly in public places
  • Escalator Etiquette: Stand on the left, walk on the right
  • Air Conditioning: Bring a light jacket - indoor spaces can be very cold
