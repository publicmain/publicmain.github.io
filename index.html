About Us

Equistar International College (ESIC) is a premier educational institution in Singapore, dedicated to providing world-class international education since 2010.

With nearly 15 years of excellence in education, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in shaping the academic futures of students from around the world. Our commitment to academic rigor, personalized attention, and holistic development sets us apart.
