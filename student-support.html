Comprehensive Student Support

We're here to help you succeed - academically, personally, and professionally

At ESIC, we believe that success extends beyond academics. Our comprehensive support services ensure you have the guidance, resources, and care needed throughout your educational journey.

New Student Orientation

Start your journey on the right foot with our comprehensive orientation program designed specifically for new students.

What's Included
  • Campus Tour: Familiarize yourself with facilities, classrooms, and resources
  • Academic Introduction: Meet your teachers and understand course expectations
  • Student Pass Guidance: Complete documentation and compliance requirements
  • Social Integration: Meet fellow students and build your network
  • Living in Singapore: Essential tips for daily life and cultural adaptation
  • Digital Resources: Access to learning platforms and online tools
Academic Counseling & Tutoring

Our academic support team provides personalized guidance to help you excel in your studies and achieve your educational goals.

Services Offered
  • One-on-One Tutoring: Subject-specific support from qualified instructors
  • Study Skills Workshops: Learn effective study techniques and time management
  • Progress Monitoring: Regular assessment reports and feedback sessions
  • Learning Plans: Customized study plans based on your learning style
  • Exam Preparation: Targeted revision sessions before major assessments
  • Assignment Support: Guidance on coursework and project submissions
Pastoral Care & Well-being

Your mental health and emotional well-being are priorities. Our qualified counselors provide confidential support throughout your teenage years.

Support Areas
  • Personal Counseling: Confidential one-on-one sessions with qualified youth counselors
  • Stress Management: Coping strategies for academic and personal pressures
  • Peer Relationships: Guidance on social integration and conflict resolution
  • Cultural Adaptation: Support for international students adjusting to Singapore
  • Mental Health Resources: Access to professional support networks when needed
  • Crisis Intervention: Immediate support during difficult times

Career Services & University Guidance

Plan your future with expert guidance on university applications, career pathways, and professional development.

What We Offer
  • University Application Support: Guidance on choosing programs and institutions
  • Personal Statement Review: Help crafting compelling application essays
  • Career Exploration: Discover career paths aligned with your interests
  • Interview Preparation: Mock interviews and feedback sessions
  • Scholarship Guidance: Identify and apply for financial aid opportunities
  • Industry Insights: Guest speakers and career talks from professionals
Masterclasses & Enrichment

Enhance your learning with specialized masterclasses delivered by subject experts and industry professionals.

  • Subject-Specific Workshops: Deep dives into complex topics
  • Critical Thinking Sessions: Develop analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Research Skills: Learn academic research methodologies
  • Presentation Skills: Effective communication and public speaking
  • Digital Literacy: Technology skills for modern learning
Learning Intervention & Appeals

We provide structured support for students facing academic challenges and a transparent process for addressing concerns.

Support Mechanisms
  • Academic Intervention: Early identification and support for struggling students
  • Personalized Action Plans: Tailored strategies to improve performance
  • Feedback Channels: Submit suggestions and concerns via dedicated email
  • Grievance Procedure: Formal process for disputes and appeals
  • Student Voice: Regular surveys to assess satisfaction and needs
