Choose Your Academic Pathway

ESIC offers comprehensive academic programs from secondary to pre-university level. Each pathway is designed with a clear progression to university admission.

SEC Preparatory

Singapore-Cambridge Certificate

Replacing O/N Level from 2027. Internally developed course with 10 subjects (compulsory + elective). Equivalent to IGCSE internationally.

  • Duration: 24 Months / 2400 Hours
  • Age: 15+ years
  • Subjects: 5-8 subjects
  • Mode: Face-to-face
View Details
GCE O-Level Intensive

12-Month Accelerated Program

Intensive one-year program preparing students for Singapore's national O-Level examinations with systematic study plans.

  • Duration: 12 Months
  • Age: 16+ years
  • Schedule: 5 days/week
  • Certificate: GCE O-Level
View Details
GCE A-Level

Premier Pre-University Program

Singapore's national pre-university qualification, recognized globally by top universities. Focuses on intensive academic preparation.

  • Duration: 24 Months
  • Age: 16+ years
  • Intakes: Jan & Jul
  • Certificate: GCE A-Level
View Details
International A Level

CIE & Edexcel Integrated Pathway

Dual exam board system. AS (Year 1) → A2 (Year 2). Choose 3-5 subjects from 10 options. Globally recognized by universities.

  • Duration: 24 Months / 2400 Hours
  • Age: 16+ years
  • Exams: May/Jun & Oct/Nov
  • Mode: Face-to-face
View Details
