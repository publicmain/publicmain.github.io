International A Level (IAL) Preparatory Program

CIE & Edexcel Dual Pathway - 24 Months

Program Overview

Our International A Level program features a dual pathway design utilizing both Cambridge International (CIE) and Pearson Edexcel examination boards. Students can select subjects across both boards for maximum flexibility.

Students typically complete 3-4 subjects over 2 years (AS â†’ A Level progression), with external examinations held in May/June and October/November each year. This globally recognized certificate is accepted by universities worldwide.

Core Subjects (Select 3-5 from Compulsory & Elective)
Available Subjects
  • English Language - Academic reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills
  • Mathematics - Mathematical modeling, problem-solving, and communication
  • Chemistry - Scientific inquiry, experimental design, and laboratory techniques
  • Physics - Theoretical understanding and practical application of physical principles
  • Biology - Biological systems, research methods, and scientific investigation
  • Economics - Critical thinking, economic analysis, and data interpretation
  • Business Studies - Business concepts, decision-making, and strategic thinking
  • Further Mathematics - Advanced mathematical concepts and applications
  • Chinese Language & Literature - Language proficiency and literary analysis
  • Computer Science - Programming, algorithms, system development lifecycle, and information ethics

Students can strategically mix subjects from both CIE and Edexcel boards to optimize their university applications.

Program Structure
Two-Year Progression
  • Year 1: AS Level (50% of full A Level)
  • Year 2: A2 Level (completes full A Level)
Examination Sessions
  • May/June Session: Annual examinations
  • October/November Session: Annual examinations

Students can sit for exams twice annually, providing flexibility for retakes or subject completion.
