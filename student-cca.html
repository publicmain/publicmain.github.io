Beyond the Classroom

Discover Your Passions Through Diverse Activities

At ESIC, we believe in developing well-rounded individuals. Our co-curricular activities (CCA) program offers diverse opportunities for students to explore interests, develop leadership skills, build friendships, and contribute to the community.

How to Join
Step 1: CCA Fair

Attend our CCA Fair at the start of each semester to explore all available activities and meet club leaders.

Step 2: Sign Up

Register for your chosen activities through the Student Portal or with the CCA coordinator.

Step 3: Participate

Attend regular sessions, contribute actively, and take on leadership roles as you grow.

Benefits of CCA Participation
  • Holistic Development: Develop skills beyond academics including leadership, teamwork, and communication
  • University Applications: Strengthen your profile with demonstrated interests and achievements
  • Social Integration: Build lasting friendships with like-minded peers
  • Stress Relief: Balance academic pressure with enjoyable activities
  • Discover Passions: Explore new interests and talents you didn't know you had
  • Leadership Opportunities: Take on organizing and mentoring roles
