How to Find Us

By MRT

Take the East West Line (Green Line) to Paya Lebar MRT Station (EW8/CC9). Exit from Exit A and walk 5 minutes to PLQ Mall. Our office is located at Level 4, Unit #04-01.

By Bus

Multiple bus services stop at Paya Lebar Road: Bus 2, 7, 13, 21, 26, 28, 30, 51, 67, 76, 135, 154, 155, 197, 851, 853, 961. Walk 3 minutes to PLQ Mall.

By Car

PLQ Mall has ample parking facilities. Enter from Paya Lebar Road and park at Level B3 or B4. Take the elevator to Level 4.
