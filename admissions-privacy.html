Privacy Policy & Personal Data Protection

Privacy Policy & Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)

Data Collection & Usage

We collect and process personal data including:

  • Identity Data: Name, IC/passport, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact Data: Address, phone, email
  • Education Data: Academic records, qualifications, transcripts
  • Financial Data: Payment information, billing details
  • Electronic Data: IP address, cookies, device information
  • Behavioral Data: Website usage, preferences
  • Marketing Preferences: Communication consent
Legal Basis for Processing
  • Contract Performance: Enrollment and education delivery
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with regulations (SEAB, ICA, SSG)
  • Legitimate Interests: Administrative and operational purposes
  • Consent: Marketing and optional communications
Your Rights
  • Access: Request copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request erasure (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Receive data in machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to automated decision-making
Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies, Google Analytics, and third-party pixels for website functionality, analytics, and marketing. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser.

Third-Party Sharing & Cross-Border Transfer

Personal data may be shared with:

  • Examination boards (SEAB, CIE, Edexcel)
  • Government agencies (ICA, MOE)
  • Service providers (IT, payment processing, insurance)
  • Universities (for applications with your consent)
Children's Data

For students under 18, we require parental/guardian consent for data collection and processing.

Data Security & Breach Notification

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. In case of a data breach, affected individuals and relevant authorities will be notified promptly.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained as required by law and regulatory bodies, typically for 7 years after course completion or as mandated by SSG/MOE.

Contact Our Data Protection Officer (DPO)

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights: dpo@esic.edu.sg
